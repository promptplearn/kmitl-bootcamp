Facilitator Run-of-Show

One room, ~2.5–3 hours, 30–65 students, two facilitators. Prom drives AI tooling, demos & the build/prompts. Chao drives business mindset, the money/grants & grading. Live site: kmitl-bootcamp.vercel.app.

Before doors open — Setup checklist

เช็กลิสต์ก่อนเริ่ม

  • Projector → board.html full-screen. The floating QR (bottom-right) sends students to vote.html — confirm it shows the live URL.
  • DELETE the 3 "DEMO ·" rehearsal teams before students arrive — 🗑 on each board card, or the cleanup command. Room must be empty at start.
  • Wi-Fi + uni AI portal open (Claude / GPT / Gemini). Remember: plain chat — web search + image gen, NO Artifacts.
  • Demo paths ready: Gemini Image, single-file HTML → preview.html (Save & share → link), or Google Stitch share link. Have one prepared example.
  • Open tabs in order: icebreaker → learn → lab → preview → submit → vote → board.
  • Hard-refresh the board once to pull the latest deploy. Reset votes / clear stage.

Total ≈ 165 min. Tight room? Trim Build to 30 and Gallery to 15. Big room (60+)? Add 5 to Submit and run pitches in two waves.

Two things not to forget

  • No Artifacts. If a student expects an interactive app from the chat, redirect: Gemini Image for visuals, or paste single-file HTML into preview.html (then Save & share for a board link).
  • Kill the DEMO teams first. The 3 "DEMO ·" cards are rehearsal only — delete them on the board before students submit, or the gallery and rubric get polluted.
